# Sunset Grill Online Ordering Marketing Plan

## Positioning

Market the feature as **faster pickup ordering for Sunset Grill**, not as an AI feature.

Customer-facing promise:

```text
Order from your phone. Customize your burger. Pay online. Pick it up hot.
```

The AI phone relay is operational infrastructure. Customers should see speed, accuracy, and convenience.

## Primary Message

```text
Skip the line. Order Sunset Grill from your phone.
Scan, customize, pay, and pick up when it is ready.
```

Supporting line:

```text
Burgers, baskets, and pickup orders made easy.
```

Trust line:

```text
Secure checkout. Fresh pickup.
```

Call to action:

```text
Scan to order
```

## In-Store QR Poster

Recommended poster copy:

```text
SKIP THE LINE

Order Sunset Grill from your phone.

Scan, customize, pay, and pick up when it is ready.

[QR CODE]

SCAN TO ORDER

Secure checkout. Fresh pickup.
```

Placement:

1. Counter/window.
2. Tabletop sign or small stand near pickup area.
3. Any printed menu or basket insert.

## First-Week Launch Offer

Best simple offer:

```text
Order online this week and get a free drink at pickup.
```

Why this offer:

* Easy for staff to fulfill.
* Low operational complexity.
* Encourages customers to try the ordering flow.
* Does not require discount-code logic before launch.

Alternate offers:

* `$1 off cheeseburger baskets ordered online.`
* `First 25 online orders get a free side.`

## Staff Script

When customers order in person, staff can say:

```text
Next time, you can scan this and order ahead from your phone.
```

For pickup customers:

```text
You can use the QR code next time and we will start the order before you get here.
```

## What Not To Lead With

Avoid leading customer-facing copy with:

* AI ordering
* automated phone relay
* Twilio
* robot caller

Those details can make the feature sound experimental. Lead with online pickup ordering.

## Launch Checklist

1. Generate final public order URL.
2. Create QR code for the URL.
3. Print counter/window poster.
4. Add QR sign near pickup area.
5. Add `Order Online` link wherever Sunset Grill is promoted.
6. Post launch announcement with URL.
7. Post cheeseburger basket photo with URL.
8. Run free-drink launch offer for one week.
9. Ask in-person customers to try scan-to-order next time.
10. Track online order count, abandoned checkout count, phone relay confirmations, and customer/staff feedback.

## After Reliability Is Proven

After one stable week, the novelty angle can be introduced lightly:

```text
Sunset Grill now has phone-powered pickup ordering.
You order online, and we handle the call-in.
```

Keep the main customer promise focused on speed and convenience.
